Hick's Law

Spotify

spotify.com

On Spotify they have applied Hick's Law where the designers have strived to minimize the number of options presented to users in order to make the decision-making process as efficient as possible.

Alignment

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/?lang=eng

From my undestanding alignment refers to the arrangement or positioning of components or data in a specific way. This is brought to the surface through the way the website for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints is desinged.

Contrast

Apple

apple.com

One example of a website that uses contrast effectively is Apple's website. The website uses a minimalistic design with a white background, which provides a high contrast with the black text and colored images used throughout the site. The large, bold headlines and text links stand out clearly against the white background, making them easy to read and navigate. The use of high-quality images and videos with vibrant colors also provides a high contrast that makes them eye-catching and attention-grabbing.
